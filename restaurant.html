Wine Chart

We focus on happy wine farmers and agriculture, and therefore our wine list is made up exclusively of wines made according to organic or biodynamic principles. Working organically and not least biodynamically is a labor-intensive process and can present many challenges when it comes to pests. It takes around 10 years to convert a vineyard that has been cultivated conventionally to become biodynamic.
