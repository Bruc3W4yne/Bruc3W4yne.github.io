- Conference and Events -

Are you going to organize a conference or course for your company? A Pour l'Effort is a conference hotel with more than 50 years of experience. We have 20 meeting and group rooms with a capacity of up to 220 people. At our prime location right outside Oslo city, with ease of access either by car, bus or train.

Our skilled employees tailor the event to your company. Our aim is to ensure that you feel looked after, both in the planning phase and during the implementation of the course and conference. We aim to give you a slightly happier day! Send us an inquiry and you will receive an offer with a price.

Our event rooms and ball rooms are famed for their luxurious feel and standards. Whether its your wedding, a confirmation, bar mitzvah or anything else you can feel at ease that the event will be one to remember. We have hosted several high end events and know how to deliver the absolute best experience possible.
